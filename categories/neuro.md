# Neuro

Datasets covering brain tissue, neural cells, neuropsychiatric conditions, or neuroimaging.

## Datasets

- [[neuropainting]] · [neuropainting](../datasets/neuropainting.md) — Cell Painting on iPSC-derived neurons with genetic perturbation.
- [[psychencode]] · [psychencode](../datasets/psychencode.md) — Multi-omic brain consortium across schizophrenia, bipolar, autism, controls.
- [[commonmind]] · [commonmind](../datasets/commonmind.md) — Postmortem DLPFC/ACC transcriptomics with matched genotype.
- [[rosmap]] · [rosmap](../datasets/rosmap.md) — Flagship AMP-AD cohort; longitudinal cognition + deep multi-omic from Rush.
- [[msbb]] · [msbb](../datasets/msbb.md) — AMP-AD Mount Sinai cohort; four matched cortical regions per donor.
- [[mayo-rnaseq]] · [mayo-rnaseq](../datasets/mayo-rnaseq.md) — AMP-AD Mayo cohort; AD vs PSP tauopathy contrast.
- [[amp-ad]] · [amp-ad](../datasets/amp-ad.md) — *(umbrella)* AD coordination program above ROSMAP/MSBB/Mayo.
- [[nacc]] · [nacc](../datasets/nacc.md) — ADRC-network dementia coordinating center (UDS clinical + neuropath).
- [[abcd]] · [abcd](../datasets/abcd.md) — Longitudinal pediatric multimodal MRI (~12k subjects), the flagship developmental cohort.
- [[abide-i]] · [abide-i](../datasets/abide-i.md) — Multi-site autism MRI benchmark (~2.1k subjects).
- [[abide-ii]] · [abide-ii](../datasets/abide-ii.md) — ABIDE extension with additional sites and modalities.
- [[hbn]] · [hbn](../datasets/hbn.md) — Transdiagnostic pediatric multimodal (~5k, MRI + EEG + clinical).
- [[ncanda]] · [ncanda](../datasets/ncanda.md) — Longitudinal adolescent neurodevelopment + alcohol use (~830 subjects).
- [[nsd]] · [nsd](../datasets/nsd.md) — 7T fMRI on ~73k natural scenes; deep within-subject visual cortex.
- [[ibc]] · [ibc](../datasets/ibc.md) — Deep within-subject task-fMRI battery across cognitive domains.
- [[narratives]] · [narratives](../datasets/narratives.md) — Story-listening fMRI (~345 subjects, 27 stories) for language-brain alignment.
- [[pgc]] · [pgc](../datasets/pgc.md) — Psychiatric GWAS meta-analyses across disorders (open summary stats).
- [[ssc]] · [ssc](../datasets/ssc.md) — Simons Simplex Collection autism quads (~2.8k families, gold-standard family design).
- [[spark]] · [spark](../datasets/spark.md) — SPARK autism cohort at scale (~250k individuals enrolled).
- [[adni]] · [adni](../datasets/adni.md) — Longitudinal AD neuroimaging and CSF biomarkers.
- [[sea-ad]] · [sea-ad](../datasets/sea-ad.md) — Single-cell AD cortex atlas with neuropath staging.
- [[allen-brain-cell-atlas]] · [allen-brain-cell-atlas](../datasets/allen-brain-cell-atlas.md) — Whole-brain single-cell + spatial reference for human and mouse.
- [[uk-biobank]] · [uk-biobank](../datasets/uk-biobank.md) — Brain MRI on ~100k subjects (aging-relevant slice).
- [[biccn]] · [biccn](../datasets/biccn.md) — *(umbrella)* BRAIN Initiative cell census program; trainable output is ABC Atlas.
- [[bican]] · [bican](../datasets/bican.md) — *(umbrella)* BICCN successor; trainable constituents still ramping.
